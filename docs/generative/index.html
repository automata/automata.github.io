Generative Design / Computational Creativity

Some types of Generative Design or Computatonal Creativity

  • Exploratory Creativity/Design

    • Represent creative assets, artfacts as feature vectors
    • The vectors form a Creative Space
    • Creativity happens by exploring through this space
      • Transversing through paths inside space
      • Generating a new feature vector close to other ones into this space
    • Interesting as application to Assisted Creation Systems

  • Style Transfer

    • Democratization of styles
    • Transfer one style into another

  • Synesthetic Transfer

    • Transfer one media into another
    • Models of different targets trained and combined together
    • Eg.: Text captions for Images
